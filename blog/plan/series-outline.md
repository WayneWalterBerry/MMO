## Key Themes Across the Series

1. **Context is limited** — everything flows from this constraint
2. **Research prevents architectural mistakes** that can't be reversed
3. **Specialists + clear boundaries = parallel execution**
4. **The human shapes the work, the AI executes it**
5. **Documentation IS architecture** for AI teams

## Wayne's Voice Notes

- Don't fabricate — use real prompts and real moments from the project
- LLMs aren't deep domain experts — research fills the gap
- Architecture reversals are HARDER with AI teams (context contamination)
- The human's contribution is questions, constraints, and vision — not code
