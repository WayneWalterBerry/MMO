### 2026-03-22T13:00: User directive — Large outdoor areas are multiple rooms (zones)
**By:** Wayne (Effe) Berry (via Copilot)
**What:** Since there is no intra-room movement (classic IF convention), large outdoor areas must be subdivided into multiple rooms that act as zones. Example: a large garden would be "south garden", "the fountain", "east lawn" — each a separate room the player moves between. Indoor rooms are naturally bounded by walls. Outdoor spaces need explicit zone boundaries.

Design team must apply this when designing outdoor levels. Every area the player can be "in" is a room, regardless of whether it has walls.
**Why:** Architectural constraint — rooms are atomic, no sub-positioning. Large spaces must be zoned.
