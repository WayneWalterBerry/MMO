### Key Design Decisions
- **Binary skills model (V1):** Have/Don't Have (no XP/proficiency tracking until V2)
- **Discovery-based acquisition:** Four paths: Find & Read manuals, Practice, NPC Teaching (future), Puzzle Solve (future)
- **Skills unlock alternatives, not replacements:** Default action always works; skill unlocks faster/additional path
- **Double dispatch gating:** Verb handlers enforce both skill AND tool requirements independently
- **Failure has consumable consequences:** Failed lock pick leaves bent-pin; failed sewing leaves tangled-mess
- **Blood writing is transgressive:** Costs 5 HP, time-limited (5 min), permanent on paper
- **Paper mutations are file-per-state:** `paper-with-writing.lua` embedded with player text
