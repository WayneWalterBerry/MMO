### Design Decisions Implemented

1. **Table-driven with lazy loading** — Definitions in `src/meta/fsms/{id}.lua`, cached after first load
2. **In-place mutation** — No object replacement; preserves containment and registry
3. **Gradual migration** — FSM check in verb handlers; fallback to old system for non-FSM objects
4. **Tick-driven auto-transitions** — Separate from verb dispatch; processes all stateful objects per-command
5. **Declarative state functions** — `on_tick` returns structured data, not side effects
