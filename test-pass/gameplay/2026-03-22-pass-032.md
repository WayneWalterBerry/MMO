## Combined Natural Phrasing

### T-016: "please could you have a look around"
**Input:** `please could you have a look around`
**Response:** `I'm not sure what you mean. Try 'help' to see what you can do, or describe what you're trying to accomplish.`
**Verdict:** ❌ FAIL
**Bug:** BUG-104
**Notes:** Politeness stripping ("please could you") should leave "have a look around", which is a known idiom for "look around". But the combined input fails. Likely the filler-stripping stage doesn't handle "could you" as a preamble, or the idiom matching runs before preamble stripping completes. Either way, a player combining politeness with an idiom should work. Contrast with T-017 which works with "I'd like to" preamble.
