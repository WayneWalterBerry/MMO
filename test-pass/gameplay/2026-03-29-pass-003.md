## Executive Summary

- **Total tests:** 30
- **Pass:** 7
- **Fail:** 19
- **Warn:** 4
- **Bugs filed:** 6 (2 CRITICAL, 3 HIGH, 1 MEDIUM)

The Grand Prize Vault is **completely unplayable**. Two critical blockers:
(1) all wyatt-world rooms are permanently dark (no `light_level`), and
(2) the room's `instances = {}` — no objects are placed. Every object
interaction fails. The puzzle verb infrastructure (`enter`, `set dial`)
doesn't exist. Options/hints produce no output. Room-level smell and
listen work; room-level feel does not.

## Conclusion

The Grand Prize Vault has **object definitions** (4 .lua files with correct
metadata, keywords, FSM states, sensory text) but **zero runtime wiring**.
No objects are placed in the room. No lighting exists. The combination-entry
verb infrastructure doesn't exist. This is a design-complete but
implementation-incomplete puzzle.

**Priority fix order:**
1. #461 — Add `light_level = 3` to all 7 rooms (unblocks vision)
2. #468 — Populate `instances` with chest, letter, streamers (unblocks objects)
3. #475 + #478 — Implement combination entry verb (unblocks puzzle solution)
4. #480 — Wire hint tiers into options system (unblocks guidance)
5. #483 — Fix feel fallback to room on_feel (polish)
