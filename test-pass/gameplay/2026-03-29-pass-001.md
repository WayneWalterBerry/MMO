## Executive Summary

**Total Tests:** 30
**Pass:** 7 | **Fail:** 19 | **Blocked:** 4
**Bugs Filed:** 4

Two CRITICAL blockers make the Beast Burger puzzle **completely unplayable**:
1. All Wyatt World rooms are dark (game starts 2 AM, no `light_level` set) — #428
2. All room `instances` are empty (objects exist as files but aren't placed in rooms) — #429

No objects can be seen, felt, taken, tasted, or assembled. The happy path, wrong-order path, eat/taste tests, and per-object sensory tests are all BLOCKED. Only room-level ambient senses (smell, listen) and navigation work.

## Conclusion

- **#428** — Rooms are dark (need `light_level = 2`)

Once those are fixed, a re-test is needed for:
- Happy path (6-step burger assembly)
- Wrong-order splat behavior
- Eat/taste all ingredients in-game
- Per-object sensory verbs (feel/smell/listen/taste on each object)

- FSM state tracking (plate advancing through steps)
