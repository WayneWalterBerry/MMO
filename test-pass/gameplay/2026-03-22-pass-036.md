## Unconsciousness Tests

### T-017: stab arm x3 → hit head → bleed out during KO
**Input:** `stab arm` × 3, then `hit head`
**Response:** After KO, 3 empty turns tick (bleeding each turn), then:
"You never wake up. The bleeding was too much." → "YOU HAVE DIED."
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — Bleeding kills during unconsciousness. Death narration is evocative.

### T-018: commands during unconsciousness
**Input:** `hit head`, then `inventory`, `look around`, `hit head`, `examine mirror`, `drop knife`, `quit`
**Response:** 5 empty ---END--- blocks during KO. After waking, all queued commands execute normally (inventory shows items, look shows room, etc.)
**Verdict:** ⚠️ WARN — Commands are silently queued, not rejected with "You're unconscious." In piped/headless mode, the game auto-ticks through unconsciousness without reading input. After waking, buffered stdin commands execute. A player in interactive mode would see the same: no prompt during KO, then normal play resumes. No rejection message is shown, but the behavior is reasonable — unconsciousness acts as a forced cutscene.

## Bug Details

### BUG-120: "reflection" not recognized as mirror keyword
**Severity:** MEDIUM
**Repro:** Type `look at my reflection` or `look at reflection` in the bedroom.
**Expected:** Triggers appearance system (mirror reflection).
**Actual:** "You don't notice anything called that nearby."
**Fix:** Add "reflection" to the vanity/mirror object's keywords list.

### BUG-121: Double death message during sleep bleedout
**Severity:** LOW
**Repro:** `stab arm` × 2, then `sleep`.
**Expected:** Single death sequence.
**Actual:** Two separate death messages fire:
  1. "You drift deeper into sleep. The pain fades. Everything fades. You never wake up. YOU HAVE DIED."
  2. "Your injuries have overwhelmed you. YOU HAVE DIED. Game over. Thanks for playing."
**Fix:** Sleep bleedout handler should set a flag that prevents the general injury-death handler from also firing.

### BUG-122: Capitalization errors in mirror appearance rendering
**Severity:** MEDIUM
**Repro:** `stab arm`, then `examine mirror`.
**Expected:** All sentences start with capital letters.
**Actual:** "A gash on your left arm. your left hand grips a small matchbox..." — Only first rendered segment is capitalized. All subsequent segments start lowercase.
**Fix:** In `appearance.lua`, capitalize the first letter of each rendered segment before joining, or ensure the join logic inserts proper sentence boundaries.

### BUG-123: Duplicate injuries not aggregated in mirror
**Severity:** LOW
**Repro:** `hit head`, wake up, `hit head` again, wake up, `examine mirror`.
**Expected:** "Two bruises on your head" or "Multiple bruises on your head."
**Actual:** "A bruise on your head and a bruise on your head."
**Fix:** In appearance rendering, group injuries by type+location and show count when > 1.
